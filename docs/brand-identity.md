# Brand Identity

## Overview

We are a software development agency that builds tools and software for small- to medium-sized businesses. Our brand identity reflects our commitment to making powerful technology accessible and understandable.

## Brand Positioning

**What we do**: Build custom tools and software solutions for SMBs
**How we're different**: We make complex technology approachable without sacrificing quality or sophistication
**Who we serve**: Small to medium-sized businesses looking to leverage custom software for growth

## Brand Values

- **Clarity**: We communicate clearly, avoiding jargon and complexity
- **Quality**: We build robust, well-crafted solutions
- **Accessibility**: We make technology understandable and usable
- **Partnership**: We work collaboratively with our clients

## Visual Identity Summary

- **Style**: Modern dark minimal - clean, spacious, content-focused with dark theme
- **Color Approach**: Dark backgrounds (black/dark grays) with vibrant accent colors (blue/green) and subtle gradients
- **Typography**: Large, bold white headlines on dark backgrounds; professional yet approachable
- **Imagery**: Dark-themed UI screenshots, clean graphics, purposeful visuals that support content
